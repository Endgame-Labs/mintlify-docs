Tasks allow users to send custom prompt outputs directly to their email inbox on a daily, weekly, or monthly schedule. 

### Create or manage existing tasks

To create or manage your existing tasks, click on the task icon in the left side navigation.

Once in the task view, you can click New Task to create a task, or click on an existing task to make updates. Endgame provides a few suggested tasks to get you started. You can enable and edit these tasks or you can create new tasks from scratch on your own. 

![tasks](/images/task-defaults-9-26-25.png)
<br/>
![tasks](/images/create-new-task-9-26-25.png)

You can disable or delete tasks anytime if you want to stop recieving fresh content. By default, tasks are sent to the email associcated with your user.

You can generate a task preview by clicking Run which will send your preview task directly to your email so you don't have to wait for your first scheduled task to send.
