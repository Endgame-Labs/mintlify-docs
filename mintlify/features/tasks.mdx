Tasks allow users to send custom prompt outputs directly to their email inbox on a daily, weekly, or monthly schedule.

### Create or manage existing tasks

To create or manage your existing tasks, click on the task icon in the left side navigation.

![tasks](/mintlify/images/tasks-8-27-25.png)

Once in the task view, you can click New Task to create a task, or click on an existing task to make updates.

You can disable or delete tasks anytime if you want to stop recieving fresh content. By default, tasks are sent to the email associcated with your user.

You can generate a task Preview and send it directly to your email so you don't have to wait for your first scheduled task to ship.

![create new task](/mintlify/images/create-new-task-8-27-25.png)
