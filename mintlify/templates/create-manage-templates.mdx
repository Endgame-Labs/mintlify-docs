<Tip>
  Want to learn more about writing and structuring templates? Check out our deep dive [here](/mintlify/templates/templates-101).
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access your Endgame configuration">
    Navigate to https://end-p1.endgame.io/configuration
  </Step>
  <Step title="Focus on Templates">
    Click "Templates" in the page navigation menu

    ![Screenshot2025 07 30at9 20 59PM Pn](/mintlify/images/Screenshot2025-07-30at9.20.59PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add, edit delete">
    - Click "Add Template" (top right of the list) to add a new template
    - Click "Edit" next to any existing template to modify or delete it

    ![Screenshot2025 07 30at9 23 36PM Pn](/mintlify/images/Screenshot2025-07-30at9.23.36PM.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
